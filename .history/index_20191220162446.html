The Filament

The Filament Bar, as the name suggests was created as a haven for those visiting to have an experience that’s unique. Some might even call unusual, and some have called it strange. We don’t know what to say to that honestly, We aim to serve good food and help create unique experiences like none other in Pune!

Flaming Masala Wings

Boldy spiced tandoori chicken wings are a game-night staple in Filament. Served with cooling coriander-cumin spiced yogurt or tangy blue cheese dressing these wings are a crowd-pleaser.

Long Island Iced Tea

A Mix of Vodka, White Rum, Gin, Tequila Orange Liqueur, Sour Mix, Coke. A Long Island iced tea is a type of alcoholic mixed drink typically made with vodka, tequila, light rum, triple sec, gin, and a splash of cola.

Anda Ghotala

Anda Ghotala is an interesting snack recipe. Made with eggs and pav bhaji masala, this interesting and quick recipe will be loved by people of all age groups. ... For making this interesting egg recipe, first of all boil 4 eggs.

Fila Twister

A Mix of Vodka, White Rum, Gin, Tequila, Orange Liqueur Sour Mix, Cranberry Juic, Pre-mixed cocktail that combines rum with apricot brandy, orange, pineapple and lime juice for a traditional Zombie cocktail. All the flavor without the high proof.
